Environments

Child-view is comprised of three image databases, an apartment environment, a house environment, and a house environment under 9 distinct lighting conditions. Each environment is furnished with a set of objects, which were sourced from a library of 3D objects using a JSON file. These databases are comprises of 14,000+ (14k) or 100,000+ (100k) images within the database.
